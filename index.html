Frequently Asked Questions

What types of pets do you sell?

We offer a variety of pets, including dogs, cats, birds, rabbits, and fish. We also provide pet accessories, food, and grooming supplies to keep your pets happy and healthy.

Can I pre-book a specific breed or pet?

Yes! If you’re looking for a particular breed or species that is currently unavailable, you can place a pre-order with us. We will notify you as soon as your desired pet becomes available. You can also request updates about new arrivals.

What are your pet shop’s delivery and shipping policies?

We offer fast and secure delivery for pet food, accessories, and supplies. Delivery times depend on your location. For live pets, we provide safe and comfortable transportation within specific areas to ensure the pet's well-being. Contact our support team for more details on shipping options.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, mobile payments, and cash on delivery (COD). Secure online payment options are also available for a hassle-free shopping experience.

Can I return or exchange pet products?

Yes, we have a return and exchange policy for unused products within a specific period. Please check our Return Policy page for details or contact our support team.
